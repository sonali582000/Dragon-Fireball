Flight of the Cursed Dragon

In the mystical land of Eldoria, a lone dragon named Drakar finds himself in a perilous journey. A curse has befallen him, and he must navigate treacherous skies, avoiding deadly arrows, and feasting on magical orbs to regain his strength.

Drakar's survival depends on your skills. Guide him through the stormy skies, avoiding the relentless arrows that seek to bring him down. Collect magical orbs scattered across the sky to nourish his power and ultimately break the curse.

Master the art of dragon flight with simple controls, Use the left and right arrow to control the dragon!
